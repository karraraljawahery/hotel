Our Story

A Legacy of Graceful Hospitality

Founded in 1987 on a secluded clifftop overlooking the Mediterranean, Aurum Resort & Spa was born from a single belief: that true luxury is not opulence, but devotion to detail.

Thirty-seven years later, our 48 rooms and 6 private villas continue to attract discerning travellers seeking tranquillity, world-class cuisine, and an intimacy that larger resorts cannot offer.
